## Email Guidelines

### Professional Emails
- Clear, concise subject line
- Proper greeting
- Purpose in first paragraph
- Supporting details in body
- Clear call to action
- Professional closing

### Casual/Internal Emails
- Friendly but professional
- Get to the point quickly
- Bullet points for lists
- Informal closing appropriate

### Follow-up Emails
- Reference previous conversation
- State purpose of follow-up
- Provide any updates
- Reiterate next steps

## Output Format
Return the email with:
- **To**: recipient
- **Subject**: subject line
- **Body**: formatted email content
